Rahul Gupta

Director
Rays Power Experts


Leveraging over 200 MW of consulting portfolio experience, an active player in the solar industry, with the commencement of 100 MW across solar park facilities....an emerging name in the solar power sector, Rays Expert Pvt. Ltd. with Mr. Rahul Gupta, MD as the pioneering visionary in this sector. Graduated from IIT Roorkee, with expertise in Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing, Mr. Gupta founded Rays Expert in 2009 and has led to the development of 230 MWs of solar plants across India. Mr. Gupta has always focused on integrating technologies & expertise across the entire solar value chain & delivering more predictable and bankable PV energy solutions that increase energy yield.

On disclosing the main idea behind his business model, Mr. Gupta says," Thermal power fetched a profit of Rs. 3/unit, bur solar power got Rs 18/unit." Maximizing value and minimizing risk has remained the key feature behind the success of Ray Experts. He has been awarded with several accolades like Asian Power Award & Global Quality Award.

Today, Mr. Gupta manages 3 projects, spanning 500 acres in Bikaner along with many upcoming investments. The 120-employee company is eyeing a turnover of Rs 750 crore in this fiscal year.

We wish him and his firm good luck for future....

NIT CONCLAVE
2nd - 4th September'16 at NIT Rourkela
Engineering India

Engineering India
An introspection to reap the best of our potentials
  • About
  • Theme
  • Objective
NIT Conclave is the gathering of the intellectual cream of the nation for the sessions of unparalleled brain-storming and exchange of ideas. It proposes great prospects of discussion on Technology, Training & Placements and Entrepreneurship. NIT Conclave is a common platform for fruitful interaction amongst the NITs - to come together and deliberate on issues pertaining to progress and development of the nation as a whole. Instead of being confined to theorize on a designated problem, delegates shall strive to address existing real issues that are hindering the nation's progress. It is a matter of immense pride for us to host the Sixth Season of the conclave at NIT Rourkela. We shall put all the required efforts to maintain the legacy our counterparts have created.
The Proposed theme of NIT Conclave ’16 is “India and Scientific research”. It is an attempt to discuss on and improve
the research scenario in the country particularity within the NIT’s.

India is in the process of rapidly expanding and reforming research in higher technical institutions and
other research institutions. It is a true fact that technical and research institutions of national importance
and lagging far behind in terms of research output. The number of research documents produced by our country
are less as compared to the 8 biggest nations. A proper research environment is required in these technical
institutions to establish itself as a center of excellence. Apart from the main theme focusing on scientific
research, other domains of discussing would be “entrepreneurship and start-up ecosystem” as well as
“Alumni Relations development”. With these in mind, it is proposed to organise the 3 day long NIT Conclave to
bring together some of the best young minds in order to address the challenge and how to go about it.
NIT Conclave is conducted annually with a vision to equip enthusiasts from these institutions of national importance with necessary skills & knowledge and to provide them with a favourable environment to pitch their ideas so that they could serve the country in a better way. We aim to build a stronger inter NIT network so that we could implement each other's ideas and establish uniformity in quality in our institutions. We, through our efforts wish to improve the quality of education so as to produce superior quality of Technocrats, thereby strengthening the Brand. Issues pertaining to NITs as a whole will be discussed with the views of identifying the obstacles that are acting as a hindrance to our growth as a brand. Notable personalities including policy and decision makers will be enlightening us on the vital aspects of innovation as well as overcoming hindrance on the road to success.
